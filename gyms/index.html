    BurnZone for gyms

    Launch a branded member app and a staff console — without building software.

    BurnZone is a multi-tenant, white-label gym platform. You get a polished member experience and an operator console that share one secure backend, branded to your gym and segregated by role and branch.

    Live means it works today. Beta is wired but maturing. Planned is on the roadmap.

    Why operators choose BurnZone

    Your brand, not ours

    Every gym is a tenant with its own name, colours and logo. Members install your app; staff sign into your console. One build serves all tenants.

    Multiple branches

    Model your real estate. Assign trainers and members to branches; scope every view, booking and report to the right location.

    Roles that match your org

    Super Admin, Branch Admin and Trainer live in one console, each seeing only what their role and branch allow.
