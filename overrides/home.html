What's in the box

A real product, not a prototype — backend, two apps, and a shared design system.

🏷️

White-label, multi-tenant

One backend serves every gym; branding & data are isolated per tenant and resolved at runtime.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Role & scope segregation

Super Admin, Branch Admin and Trainer share one console — each sees only what their role and branch allow.

📅

Bookings end-to-end

Trainer availability → generated slots → member booking → completion, with cancellations and status tracking.

🍎

Nutrition & goals

Calorie/macro logging, daily targets derived from a member's goal, weigh-ins and consistency streaks.

📱

One codebase, every channel

Flutter member & admin apps for iOS, Android and web, over a shared Dart core (API, models, auth, theme).

🔒

Secure by default

JWT auth with silent refresh, persisted sessions, RBAC on every route, CORS allow-listing and audit logging.

How it works

From sign-up to a session booked — the whole loop is wired.

1

Provision a gym

A tenant is created with branding, branches and a plan. Staff and members are invited.

2

Operators set up

Branch admins add trainers & branches; trainers set availability, which generates bookable slots.

3

Members engage

Members train, log meals against a goal, and book trainer sessions at their branch.

4

Everyone tracks

Members see streaks & progress; operators see KPIs, bookings and usage in the console.
