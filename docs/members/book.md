# Book

Book a 1:1 session with a trainer at your branch — the whole flow is live.

## How booking works <span class="bz-badge live">Live</span>

1. **Your branch is resolved automatically** from your membership.
2. **Pick a trainer** from the bookable trainers at that branch.
3. **Choose a day** — the next 7 days are right there.
4. **Tap an available slot** (shown in your local time) and confirm, with optional
   notes.
5. Done — it appears under **My bookings**, and the slot is taken.

## My bookings <span class="bz-badge live">Live</span>

See your upcoming and past sessions with their status — `confirmed`, `completed`,
`cancelled`. Need to change plans? **Cancel** a confirmed booking in a tap and the
slot frees up again.

## Behind the scenes

Trainers set their availability in the console, which generates real, bookable slots.
When you book, the slot is reserved atomically so two members can't grab the same
time. Cancellations and completions flow back to the gym's console.

!!! note "Privacy by design"
    You can see the *bookable* trainers at **your** branch — not the gym's full staff
    roster. That's a deliberate, enforced boundary (see
    [Roles & access](../gyms/roles-access.md)).
