# Eat

Nutrition tracking that takes seconds, tied to your goal.

## Daily summary <span class="bz-badge live">Live</span>

The Eat tab shows **today's calories against your target**, how many you have
remaining, and your macro split — **protein, carbs, fat**. Your target isn't a guess:
it comes from your active [goal](progress.md).

## Log a meal <span class="bz-badge live">Live</span>

Tap **Log meal**, pick a meal type (breakfast / lunch / dinner / snack), add a short
description and calories (and macros if you like). Your summary and today's list
update instantly.

## Today's meals <span class="bz-badge live">Live</span>

Everything you've logged today is listed with its meal type, description and calories,
so you can see where the day went at a glance.

## Streaks keep you honest <span class="bz-badge live">Live</span>

Logging consistently builds a **streak** — a simple, visible nudge that's one of the
best predictors of sticking with it. Your current and longest streaks show on Home.
