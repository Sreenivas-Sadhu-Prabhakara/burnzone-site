# BurnZone for members

**Everything for your fitness journey — in one app.**

BurnZone's member app brings your workouts, nutrition, trainer bookings and progress
together. It's a real, native-feeling app on **iOS, Android and the web** — fast,
clean, and built around staying consistent.

## The five tabs

<div class="bz-grid" markdown>

<div class="bz-feature" markdown>
### :material-home: Home
Your daily snapshot: calories vs target, your **streak** and goal progress — with
quick actions to log a meal, browse workouts or book a session.
</div>

<div class="bz-feature" markdown>
### :material-dumbbell: Train
A library of workouts and **curated programs** — duration, difficulty, estimated
burn and a video link to follow along.
</div>

<div class="bz-feature" markdown>
### :material-food-apple: Eat
Log meals in seconds; see today's total against your **daily target** and your
protein / carbs / fat breakdown.
</div>

<div class="bz-feature" markdown>
### :material-calendar-check: Book
Browse a trainer's available slots at your branch and **book a 1:1 session** —
manage and cancel from one place.
</div>

<div class="bz-feature" markdown>
### :material-trophy: Track
Set a **goal**, record weigh-ins, and watch your streak grow. Small, visible wins
that keep you going.
</div>

</div>
