# BurnZone for gyms

**Launch a branded member app and a staff console — without building software.**

BurnZone is a multi-tenant, white-label gym platform. You get a polished member
experience and an operator console that share one secure backend, branded to your
gym and segregated by role and branch.

<span class="bz-badge live">Live</span> means it works today. <span class="bz-badge beta">Beta</span> is wired but maturing. <span class="bz-badge planned">Planned</span> is on the roadmap.

## Why operators choose BurnZone

<div class="bz-feature" markdown>
### :material-palette: Your brand, not ours
Every gym is a **tenant** with its own name, colours and logo. Members install

### :material-store: Multiple branches
Model your real estate. Assign trainers and members to branches; scope every view,
